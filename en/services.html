What we do

Our services

Specialised, tailored support for MAH holders, sponsors and pharmaceutical companies. From pharmacovigilance to Kaizen consulting, throughout the drug lifecycle.

01

Pharmacovigilance

We provide a complete pharmacovigilance service for EU MAH holders, ensuring compliance with GVP guidelines and EMA/AIFA regulations. Our expert team manages the entire safety reporting and evaluation cycle.

Request a consultation
  • EU-QPPV — EU-resident Qualified Person for Pharmacovigilance
  • PSMF — Drafting and maintenance of the Pharmacovigilance System Master File
  • ICSRs / SAEs / SUSARs — Collection, assessment and transmission to Eudravigilance within regulatory timelines
  • Eudravigilance — Registration and management of the European safety reporting system
  • XEVMPD / Art. 57 — Management of medicinal product data in Extended EudraVigilance
  • Safety databases — Implementation and management of safety data collection databases
  • PSUR / PBRER — Drafting of Periodic Safety Update Reports and Periodic Benefit-Risk Evaluation Reports
  • RMP — Risk Management Plan: drafting, implementation and updates
  • Signal analysis — Detection and assessment of new safety signals per GVP Module IX
  • EMA/AIFA submissions — Managing submissions to competent authorities within regulatory deadlines
  • DSUR — Development Safety Update Reports for medicines in clinical development
02

Periodic safety reports

Drafting PSURs, PBRERs and RMPs requires in-depth knowledge of EMA regulations and scientific literature. Our team delivers high-quality, complete reports aligned with regulatory requirements — with a zero critical findings track record.

Request a consultation
03

Medical Information

The medical information service is a regulatory obligation for pharmaceutical companies. MAC supports companies in managing this service, providing accurate, scientifically sound responses in compliance with applicable regulations.

Request a consultation
  • Scientific Service Officer — Support or coverage of the mandatory Scientific Service Officer role
  • MI request management — Responding to medical information requests from HCPs and patients
  • MI database — Implementation and management of medical information request tracking databases
  • Standard Response Letters — Drafting standardised response letters for frequently asked questions
  • Literature searches — Systematic searches of scientific literature to support medical information
  • SOP drafting — Standard Operating Procedures for pharmacovigilance and medical information processes
  • Audit support — Preparation and assistance during internal audits and regulatory authority inspections
  • Gap analysis — Assessment of existing pharmacovigilance system and identification of improvement areas
  • CAPA management — Management of corrective and preventive actions following audits and inspections
  • Training — Staff training on pharmacovigilance, GVP and EMA/AIFA regulations

Quality & Compliance

Regulatory compliance is the foundation of all pharmaceutical activity. We support companies in implementing and maintaining robust quality management systems, audit preparation and non-conformance management.

05

Kaizen Consulting

Kaizen — literally "change for the better" — is the continuous improvement philosophy that permeates all MAC activities. We bring this culture into client companies to optimise processes, reduce waste and improve overall quality.

Request a consultation
  • Lean Thinking — Analysis and optimisation of workflows using a Lean approach
  • Kaizen Events — Intensive workshops to identify and implement rapid process improvements
  • Value Stream Mapping — Mapping value streams to identify waste and bottlenecks
  • Pharmaceutical KPIs — Definition and monitoring of key performance indicators in PV processes
  • Culture of change — Training and coaching to create an organisation oriented towards continuous improvement
