FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Answers to the most common questions about our services and how to work with us.

Pharmacovigilance is the science and activities relating to the detection, assessment, understanding and prevention of adverse effects of medicines. It is mandatory for all Marketing Authorisation Holders (MAHs) under Regulation (EU) No 726/2004 and Directive 2001/83/EC, as amended by Regulation (EU) No 1235/2010.

The EU Qualified Person for Pharmacovigilance (EU-QPPV) is responsible for implementing and maintaining the pharmacovigilance system on behalf of the MAH. It is mandatory for all EU MAH holders and the person must reside within the EU. MAC provides an outsourced EU-QPPV service for companies of any size.
