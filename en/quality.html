Ethics & Compliance

Quality & Ethics Code

We operate with integrity, transparency and rigour in every activity. The principles guiding our work are not mere statements of intent — they are the concrete foundation of every professional relationship we build.

Our commitment

Ethics and Compliance Code

Our consulting firm operates in support of pharmaceutical companies with the aim of creating value in full compliance with rules, ethics and quality standards required by a highly regulated sector.

Regulatory Compliance

We are committed to respecting and promoting applicable Good Practices (GxP), national and international regulations of the pharmaceutical sector, and quality, traceability and control standards.

Our consulting activities are aimed at supporting clients in complying with regulatory requirements, inspections and applicable legal obligations, avoiding approaches that could compromise their compliance.

Integrity, Anti-Corruption and Transparency

The Company promotes a culture of integrity, fairness and transparency in all its activities. Every professional and commercial relationship is managed ethically, documented and in compliance with applicable regulations.

The Company does not tolerate behaviour that could compromise the independence, credibility or reputation of itself or its clients. Adequate measures are adopted to prevent improper practices, conflicts of interest, favouritism and any form of behaviour not in line with the principles of fairness and legality.

Professional Independence

We operate independently from our clients, avoiding conflicts of interest that could compromise the objectivity of our analyses and recommendations.

Client Relationships

Relationships with clients are based on the principles of collaboration, transparency, reliability and mutual accountability. The Company operates as a professional partner, providing expertise, methodologies and best practices to support continuous improvement of processes, quality and regulatory compliance.

Consulting activities are carried out on the basis of information, data and statements provided by the client. The Company does not carry out operational activities or direct control over client processes, unless otherwise agreed contractually. The implementation of recommendations and compliance with regulations remain the client's responsibility.

Confidentiality and Data Protection

We protect clients' technical, scientific and commercial information with the utmost confidentiality. Personal and corporate data are processed in compliance with current data protection regulations, including the provisions of the European GDPR Regulation, adopting adequate technical and organisational measures to ensure their security, integrity and confidentiality.

Quality and Responsibility

Our consulting activities are guided by four fundamental principles:

  • Quality — methodological rigour, technical expertise and attention to real value for the client
  • Accuracy — analyses, assessments and recommendations based on reliable and verifiable data
  • Traceability — clear, reconstructible and controllable processes and documentation
  • Responsibility — awareness of the impact of our activities on clients and the sector

Partner and Supplier Relationships

The Company builds its relationships with partners and suppliers on the principles of legality, quality, transparency and responsibility. Collaborations are based on fairness, reliability and mutual respect, with the aim of creating a network of parties that share high professional, ethical and compliance standards, particularly in the context of the pharmaceutical sector.
