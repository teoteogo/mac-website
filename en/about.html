Our story

About us

Since 2001, Medical Affairs Consulting has supported pharmaceutical companies with expertise, passion and an approach centred on continuous improvement.

Since 2001

A history of excellence and continuous growth

Medical Affairs Consulting was founded in 2001 by a group of pharmaceutical professionals united by a simple but ambitious idea: to offer pharmaceutical companies a reliable, competent, quality-oriented partner for pharmacovigilance and medical affairs management.

Over the years, MAC has consolidated its presence in the Italian and European market, building a multidisciplinary team of over 30 professionals — physicians, pharmacists, biologists, engineers and IT consultants — all united by a passion for excellence and adherence to Kaizen values.

Today MAC is the reference point for numerous Italian and international pharmaceutical companies that entrust us with the complete management of their pharmacovigilance, medical information and quality systems.

2001

MAC founded

Medical Affairs Consulting established in Saronno (VA), with an initial team dedicated to pharmacovigilance.

2010

Service expansion

Integration of medical information services and launch of first Kaizen activities in internal processes.

2018

Team growth

Team exceeds 20 professionals. Launch of Kaizen consulting for external clients and adoption of advanced IT tools.

Today

30+ professionals, 0 EMA findings

A team of excellence serving Italian and European pharmaceutical companies, with zero critical EMA findings.

Our identity

Mission & Values

Our Mission

To ensure excellence in Pharmacovigilance and Medical Information, and to promote a culture of continuous Change through Kaizen throughout the drug lifecycle.

We believe patient safety is the highest value in the pharmaceutical sector and that every activity we perform should contribute to this goal.

Our Values

We value people as our most important asset. Their curiosity and energy are the intangible capital that drives innovation and sustainable growth.

  • Excellence in every activity
  • Tailored approach for each client
  • Transparency and professional integrity
  • Continuous improvement (Kaizen)
  • Collaboration and team spirit
